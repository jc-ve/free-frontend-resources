Laurence Llamas

Laurence Llamas is a filmmaker/based in Manila, Philippines, and hails from his roots in Dagupan, Pangasinan. He gained early recognition by showcasing his photography talents in different photo exhibits, including Kalye Dagupan (2017) and the Dagupan Photo (2021). With a deep-seated interest and passion for the arts, He eventually moved to Far Eastern University to study digital cinema. There, he actively engaged in various student-led productions, honing his skills as a writer and producer.

One of his current and most significant milestones came with the creation of his best film to date, "Ang Pagtangis ng mga Aninong Umiindak sa Hangin" (2023), which garnered critical acclaim and numerous awards within local and prestigious film festivals including SINEPIYU XV (2023) and GAWAD ALTERNATIBO 2023.
